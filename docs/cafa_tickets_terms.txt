7. PLATFORM FEES AND ORGANIZER PAYOUTS

Platform Fee: Cafa Tickets charges a Platform Fee of 5% on every paid ticket sold through the Platform.
This fee is automatically deducted from each ticket sale at the time of purchase before the net proceeds
are credited to your Organizer Wallet. The Platform Fee covers payment processing costs, platform
infrastructure, and the tools and services Cafa Tickets provides to Organizers.

Organizer Wallet: When a Consumer purchases a ticket to your event, Cafa Tickets processes the payment
and credits the net amount — that is, the ticket price minus the 5% Platform Fee — to your Organizer
Wallet. For example, if a ticket sells for GH₵ 100, GH₵ 95 is credited to your Wallet and GH₵ 5 is
retained by Cafa Tickets as the Platform Fee. Your Organizer Wallet balance is displayed in real time in
the Revenue section of your dashboard within the Cafa Tickets app.

Withdrawals: As an Organizer, you may request a withdrawal of your available Organizer Wallet balance at
any time through your dashboard. There is no need to wait until after the event to access your earnings.
The minimum withdrawal amount is GH₵ 30. Withdrawal requests are typically disbursed to your registered
payment profile within 1 to 3 business days, subject to identity verification and any applicable
compliance checks.

Pending and On-Hold Funds: A portion of your Wallet balance may be marked as pending or on-hold. Pending
funds represent proceeds from recent ticket sales that are still within the payment processing window.
On-hold funds may be reserved by Cafa Tickets in instances of suspected fraud, disputed transactions,
chargebacks, or violations of these Terms. Cafa Tickets will notify you of any holds and will endeavour
to resolve them promptly.

No Additional Withdrawal Fees: Cafa Tickets does not charge any additional fees to process your
withdrawal requests. The only deduction from your ticket sale proceeds is the 5% Platform Fee applied
at the time of each individual sale.

Free Events: Cafa Tickets does not charge a Platform Fee on tickets to free events (i.e., events with a
GH₵ 0.00 ticket price). There is no cost to list or publish a free event on the Platform.

Tax Obligations: Organizers are solely responsible for determining, collecting, reporting, and remitting
